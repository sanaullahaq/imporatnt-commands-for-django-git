1. To exclude all files ending in .pyc use:
   *.pyc
2. To exclude all files ending in .log4j use:
   *.log*
   Here, 4j is just a representation. It can be any letter or number.
3. To exclude all files in a specific path, such as the application 
   venv directory. This will ignore the venv directory and everything 
   within it.
   /venv/*
